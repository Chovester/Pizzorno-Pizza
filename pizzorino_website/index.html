Vegetarien Krabby Pizza

  • baby spinache
  • shredded mozzarela,
  • canned anchovies,
  • halved cherry
  • tomatoes
  • pitted kalamata olives
  • and sliced almonds

    • Preheat the oven to 500 degrees Fahrenheit with a rack in the upper third of the oven. If using a baking stone or baking steel, place it on the upper rack. Prepare the dough according to the recipe instructions. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the two pizzas, leaving about 1 inch bare around the edges. Evenly distribute the spinach on top of the sauce, followed by the cheese. Top the pizzas with artichoke, bell pepper, red onion, tomatoes, olives, and almonds (if using). Bake pizzas individually on the top rack until the crust is golden and the cheese is golden and bubbly, about 10 to 12 minutes (or significantly less if using a baking stone/steel—keep an eye on it). Transfer pizzas to a cutting board and garnish with fresh basil, red pepper flakes, and Parmesan, if using. Slice and serve!
